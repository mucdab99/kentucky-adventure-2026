Blue Heron Loop Trail

Big South Fork’s greatest mix of hiking, history, geology, and river views.

Why It’s Worth It

The National Park Service describes Blue Heron Loop as a moderate 6.5-mile hike combining history, geology, and scenery. It passes old mining features, climbs to gorge-rim overlooks, and goes through the natural cliff opening called “Cracks in the Rocks.”

6.5 milesModerateHistory + Views

Do Not Miss

  • Blue Heron Mining Community
  • Devil’s Jump overlook
  • Cracks in the Rocks
  • River gorge views
