Activity Deep Dive

Auxier Ridge → Courthouse Rock → Double Arch

The classic postcard hike with big ridge views and sandstone architecture.

Why It’s Iconic

This is the scenic showcase: ridge walking, Courthouse Rock, valley views, and the impressive Double Arch formation. Kentucky Hiker calls the longer version one of the best loops in Kentucky, with a 7.7-mile route if you include spurs.

~6–7.7 milesModerateClassic RRG

Best Stops

  • Auxier Ridge overlooks
  • Courthouse Rock viewpoint
  • Double Arch
  • The Observatory above Double Arch
