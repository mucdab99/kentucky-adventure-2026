Trail Maps & Navigation

Open these links on Wi‑Fi before leaving the cabin. Download maps in AllTrails or Gaia GPS and screenshot the trail overview and parking area.

Before Leaving the Cabin

  • Download the route in AllTrails or Gaia GPS.
  • Save a Google Maps offline area for Frenchburg, Slade, Stanton, Lexington, and Big South Fork / Stearns.
  • Screenshot each trail map, trailhead, and parking area.
  • Assume cell service may be spotty in Red River Gorge and Big South Fork.
  • Carry a battery pack and keep one phone with extra charge as the backup navigator.

Offline App Notes

AllTrails: Open the route, tap download/offline map if you have that feature available, and screenshot the map either way.

Gaia GPS: Save the area/route for offline use before leaving Wi‑Fi.

Google Maps: Tap your profile picture → Offline maps → Select your own map, then cover the cabin, Slade, Stanton, Lexington, and the Blue Heron area.

Some app features require a paid subscription. Screenshots are the universal backup.
