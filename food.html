Food, Groceries & Restaurant Game Plan

Practical grocery stops near the cabin, plus the best-regarded Red River Gorge food options for hikers, kids, and tired adults.

Big stock-up

Kroger — Morehead, KY

Best bet for a full grocery run on arrival or before heading deep into the weekend. Use this for breakfast supplies, trail lunches, snacks, drinks, and anything you do not want to hunt for later.

Closest emergency runs

Dollar General — Frenchburg

Good for quick essentials, forgotten toiletries, snacks, drinks, and basic cabin supplies. Dollar General lists a Frenchburg location at 627 Main St.

Classic Gorge stop

Miguel's Pizza — Slade

The iconic climber/hiker stop. Best used as Friday lunch between the two big hike blocks.

Open Map Search
Best all-around dinner candidate

Red River Rockhouse — Campton

Highly regarded local option. Their own site describes an eclectic American cafe with grass-fed burgers, tacos, burritos, salads, tap beer, wine, and bourbon.

Website
Pre-hike coffee / breakfast

Daniel Boone Coffee Shop — Slade

Strong candidate for coffee, breakfast sandwiches, baked goods, smoothies, and lunch items before or between Gorge activities.

Other good names to check

RedPoint BBQ / Thatcher Barbecue / La Cabaña / Sky Bridge Station

Worth checking hours and location depending on the exact day. Around the Gorge, hours can be seasonal and weekend-heavy, so verify before committing the whole hungry crew.

Suggested Food Strategy

Thursday

Stock up in Morehead or Frenchburg. Easy cabin dinner after sunset.

Friday

Pack breakfast + trail food. Miguel's at lunch. Red River Rockhouse or cabin dinner after Jump Rock.

Sunday

Cabin breakfast, packed lunch for Blue Heron, easy grill/fire-pit dinner back at the cabin.

Cabin Grocery Checklist

  • Coffee, creamer, breakfast food
  • Sandwich stuff / wraps
  • Fruit, salty snacks, protein bars
  • Electrolyte packets / sports drinks
  • Water / refill jugs if needed
  • Easy grill dinner
  • S'mores or fire-pit snacks
  • Paper towels, trash bags, foil, zip bags
  • Bug spray and sunscreen backup
