Jump Rock / Sheltowee Connector

The river cooldown: short walk, swimming hole, and optional rock-jump energy.

Why It’s Fun

After a long hiking day, this is the perfect cool-down stop. Swim, sit by the river, and let the day unwind. Treat the jumping part as optional and conditions-dependent.

Short WalkSwimmingCooldown

Safety Notes

  • Water depth changes.
  • Check for submerged rocks/logs.
  • Jumping is optional, not required.
  • Water shoes help.
  • No hero moves after a 10+ mile hiking day.
