Don't Forget

Packing & Safety

The boring page that prevents bad moods, blisters, dehydration, and “who forgot the charger?” moments.

Trail Kit

  • Hiking shoes / trail runners
  • 2–3 liters water per person
  • Electrolytes
  • Snacks and lunch
  • Rain shell
  • Hat / sunglasses
  • Bug spray
  • Sunscreen
  • Small first aid kit
  • Phone battery pack

River / Festival Kit

  • Water shoes or sandals
  • Towels
  • Dry clothes
  • Clear bag for festival if required
  • Ear protection
  • Meeting point plan
  • Cash/card
  • ID
Safety: Indian Staircase and unofficial Gorge routes can involve scrambling and exposure. Avoid wet rock. At Jump Rock, check water depth and submerged hazards before anyone considers jumping.
