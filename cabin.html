Cabin Details

Check-in, directions, Wi-Fi, hot tub rules, wildlife reminders, and checkout notes.

  • Check-in: Any time after 4:00 PM.
  • Door code: 0684
  • Relocking: Push the lock button.
  • Checkout: 11:00 AM.

Wi-Fi

  • Network: Star way 5 or Star way 2.4
  • Password: long2021

Critical Driving Warning

Print directions in case there is no cell service.

Do not use Highway 713 / Indian Creek Road to get to the cabin. It can send you on a wild goose chase. Also avoid Bear Branch Road and Amos Cut Road / Patrick Cemetery Road.

Best Directions

  1. Use Google Maps to navigate to 822 Forest View Dr., Frenchburg, KY 40322.
  2. Watch for Amos Ridge Rd., a sharp right turn off McCausey Ridge Rd.
  3. Look for the sign that says Hwy 3339 or Cabin Rentals.
  4. Once on Amos Ridge Rd., continue about 4.1 miles to Forest View Dr.
  5. Forest View Dr. is a gravel road with a blue road sign on the left.
  6. A pointer sign with cabin names will be on the post.
  7. Moondance is the third cabin on the right, about 1/2 mile in.

Hot Tub Rules

Please shower before getting in the hot tub. Body lotions and oils can clog the filter.

Important: There is a $100 charge if the tub has to be drained and the filter replaced because lotions or oils were not washed off.

Cabin-in-the-Woods Notes

  • Insects, wasps, flies, and other critters may find their way inside.
  • Watch for snakes when walking or hiking outside.
  • There have been bear sightings in the area.
  • Do not leave food outside the cabin.

Rain / Fireplace / Fire Pit

  • There is a recurring leak in the primary bathroom near the vent during heavy rain. If it happens, place a bowl under the leak area and ask for extra towels if needed.
  • The gas fireplace is operational October 1 through March 31 only.
  • Firewood delivery: Call Caz Carter at 321-443-3378. Usually $7 per bundle.

Grill & Parking

  • Parking is in front of the cabin.
  • Charcoal grill is to the right of the cabin.
  • Bring your own charcoal.

Checkout Checklist

  • Place dishes in dishwasher and start the cycle.
  • Place garbage in the outside container.
  • Leave sheets on the beds.
  • That’s about it.
