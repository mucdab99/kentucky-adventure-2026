Activity Deep Dive

Indian Staircase

The adventure hike: sandstone steps, scrambling, cliffs, and big Red River Gorge energy.

Why It’s Iconic

Indian Staircase is the “kids will remember this forever” hike: natural sandstone steps, hands-on scrambling, ledges, and huge views. Kentucky Hiker describes the route as iconic, beautiful, and adventurous, with Class 2/3 scrambling and intermediate navigation.

~4.15 miles~1,191 ft gainModerate–Strenuous

Dad's Notes

  • Do first while fresh.
  • Avoid if wet, icy, or stormy.
  • Keep everyone away from cliff edges.
  • Download maps offline.
  • Make this the “team adventure” of the trip.
