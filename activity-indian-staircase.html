Indian Staircase

The adventure hike: sandstone steps, scrambling, cliffs, and big Red River Gorge energy.

Why It’s Iconic

Indian Staircase is the “kids will remember this forever” hike: natural sandstone steps, hands-on scrambling, ledges, and huge views. This is the right one to do first while everyone is fresh.

~4–5 milesScrambleModerate–Strenuous

Dad's Notes

  • Do first while fresh.
  • Avoid if wet, icy, or stormy.
  • Keep everyone away from cliff edges.
  • Download maps offline.
  • Make this the team adventure of the trip.
